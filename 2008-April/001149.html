Hello Wolfgang,

I wouldn't break the loop right after first non-existant channel. If the
first channel of a card is broken but not the second channel, the driver
must detect the second working channel.

I'm going to remove the "removing it" message and add the information
which IRQ was claimed.

Stay tuned for the patch. ;-)

Sebastian
