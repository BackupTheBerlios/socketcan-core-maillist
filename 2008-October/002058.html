Hi,
give a look at drivers/net/enc28j60.c drivers. It's a network driver
with SPI similar to mcp251x.

I don't remember exactly every detail, but pay attention at the comment
in enc28j60_tx_timeout, may be it's useful.

static void enc28j60_tx_timeout(struct net_device *ndev)
{
	struct enc28j60_net *priv = netdev_priv(ndev);

	if (netif_msg_timer(priv))
		dev_err(&ndev->dev, DRV_NAME " tx timeout\n");

	ndev->stats.tx_errors++;
	/* can't restart safely under softirq */
	schedule_work(&priv->restart_work);
}
