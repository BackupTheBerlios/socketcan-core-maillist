'bitrate' definately yes. 'baud' is the wrong unit, see 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Baud

I would suggest to 'bitperiod' as the most appropriate name. 'time' generally 
means a point on the time line, while 'period' is the amount of time between 
two points.
