No. The FT2232/SJA100 combination from the FT2232 datasheet uses the FT2232
in MCU Host Bus Emulation Mode. By writing 2 or 3 Bytes to the USB
device a 8051 like bus cycle is triggered. The result from a read cycle on
the "microcontroller" bus can then be read from the USB device. 

Probably doomed to be slow as this ping-pong with read and write on the usb
bus has to happen in the 8 kHz frame of the USB bus, as I understand USB.
But, you guess , I have some of these boards in the drawer too :-)
