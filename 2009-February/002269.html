I think one of the main reasons was, that the CAN subsystem is a quite 
closed eco-system in the kernel. So when you enable the CAN network 
layer stuff you are directly guided to the CAN drivers. Not everyone is 
aware about the technical background that the CAN subsystem deals with 
network device drivers. And so he does not get lost in tons of IP 
enabled devices. E.g. the bluetooth and IRDA drivers can also be found 
the networking section.
