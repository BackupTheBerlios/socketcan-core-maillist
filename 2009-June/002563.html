Hello,

here is brief status of our kernel inclusion effort. The CAN device
driver interface plus drivers for the SJA1000, sja1000_platform,
sja1000_of_platform, ems_pci and kvaser_pci, are now in David Miller's
"net-next-2.6" branch and will show up in 2.6.31 soon. Please note that
the sja1000_of_platform driver uses different bindings than we had so
far and the SJA1000 drivers do not misuse netdev->base_addr any more.

The next step would be to get the SVN branch "branches/netlink" in sync
with "net-next-2.6" and sooner than later move it to trunk. Oliver,
could you please re-introduce ND2D() first, also to get the MSCAN and
AT91 driver compiled. Then I would apply the recent "net-next-2.6" changes.

Wolfgang.
