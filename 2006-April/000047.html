>> For a better compatibility with vanilla kernel,
>> I rearrange your kernel/2.6 subtree.

>> Comments? Objections?

> As Marc already pointed out, the current structure was chosen to make
> compiling outside of the kernel tree easier.  I suggest not to change
> it until we get the stuff into the mainstream kernel.  The current
> layout makes it possible to untar, cd, and make without patching any
> existing tree.
It still possible to build drivers and can-core as external modules,
only one simple addition needed in Makefiles:

ifneq ($(KBUILD_EXTMOD),)
EXTRA_CFLAGS += -I$(KBUILD_EXTMOD)/../include
endif


P.S. Btw, $(SUBDIRS) now deprecated, use M=<module dir> instead.
