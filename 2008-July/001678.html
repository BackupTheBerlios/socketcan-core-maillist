I feel that the method & the content have different constraints.
Although replaceable tables (=content), or raw registers may be alternatives that solve
some users' problem, the sysfs interface (=method) poses a problem on such ways.
I would think that such things are no long-term solutions, but time-critical situations may
make such inevitable.
Therefore, an interface (=method) that could be extended without breaking "do's & don'ts"
would be nice, but sysfs is limited to key=value pairs.
