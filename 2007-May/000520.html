Robert Schwebel wrote:
> What about using an svn checked in quilt stack as the primary source,
> now that the process of mainlining has started?

Hm - as you stated correctly the process of mainlinig has just 
*started*. The posted patch series is split up e.g. for the 
net/can/Kconfig file. As the SocketCAN core (can.ko and friends) should 
be really stable and it is also used by people that are not using the 
bleeding edge kernel versions, the current approach looks reasonable for 
me at least until the stuff finally went into the mainline kernel.

The creation of patches for different kernel versions is working very 
well and this looks like the major current use-case for me. The patches 
for the netdev ML are currently just *one* output path.

Even if the stuff went into the mainline kernel, we should IMO support 
older kernels. That's not that big effort.
