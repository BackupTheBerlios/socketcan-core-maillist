Andrey,

On Thu, May 25, 2006 at 06:44:55PM +0400, Andrey Volkov wrote:
> AFIK all currently produced (widespread) chips could be programmed
> properly by this canonical values (roughly speaking, BTR0 =
> prop_seg+phase_seg1, and BTR1 = phase_seg2), but the reverse situation
> is not true.

The idea in our concept was to have "just two arbitrary values" and let
the user decide what they mean, according to the documentation of the
driver. In the standard case this could be the phase values from the can
spec, but in special cases the values could just provide what the chip
can handle.
