> as stated in Documentation/networking/can.txt Chapter 3.3, it makes
> sense to allow 'normal' non-root users to access the CAN bus (e.g.
> via the CAN_RAW socket). In multi-user systems (e.g. your

I second this, because capabilities as implemeted in Linux to date
are a pain in the ass.

Anyway, I don't think that hardcoding a user(id)/group(id) would be a
good Idea. How about using a method modeled after Realtime LSM (->
http://sourceforge.net/projects/realtime-lsm/)? On the first glance
its looks pretty easy to extend realtime lsm for CAP_NET_RAW.
