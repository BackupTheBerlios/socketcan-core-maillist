In other situations (non sysfs related) I have had good success with a
clear split between the roles of attributes.
Suggested groups:
-> Capabilities
-> Actual status
-> Configuration

In this way it is possible to query the capabilities no matter
the actual configuration - and you do nto mix capabilities with 
what is configured.

A general comment that may apply better to other entries than restart-ms,
but wanted to bring it up.
