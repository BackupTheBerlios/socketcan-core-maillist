Yes, that's neither necessary nor desirable. One person can be ill, on
holidays, or occupied by other work. IMO, the important thing is "one
place", not "one person". There should be one and only one repository
(be it git, svn, quilt queue or whatever) where one can get the latest
state of socketcan stuff. It doesn't make much difference if I cc one
or two or three maintainers, but if I don't know where my patch goes
and who applies it to which tree, that's a real PITA...
