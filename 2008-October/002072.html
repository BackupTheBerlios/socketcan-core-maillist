Hi Christian,

just a view remarks on publishing patches:

- Add some comments to the patch header on what the patch does fix,
  enhance, etc. and why.
- Send a series of patches if various different issues are addressed.
- Do not forget your "Signed-off-by" line.
- Mark new patch versions with a suffix, e.g. [PATCH v2].
