Hi Adil,
I'd recommend downloading the current mainline testing kernel
2.6.25-rc3 (download stable 2.6.24 and apply patch-2.6.25-rc3). Note
that this doesn't include any drivers at the moment. If you need such a
chip driver (e.g. for SJA1000), you have to extract it from socketcan
SVN at berlios. If you give us more details about what you're trying to
do (use an existing driver or write your own?), we can certainly give
you more advice.
