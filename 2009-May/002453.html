Hello,

after submitting the at91_can driver I just wanted to submit platform activation part of this driver to the ARM linux tree. Except .name = "at91_can" it has nothing to do with socketcan itself and can be used for example by can4linux project provided they would support platform device driver model. If it will be accepted then ARM part will be ready for including socketcan into mainline kernel. 

Unfortunately the patch name I've chosen was misleading and caused following discussion http://lists.arm.linux.org.uk/lurker/message/20090409.093528.b9704972.en.html

The patch itself can be found here http://www.arm.linux.org.uk/developer/patches/viewpatch.php?id=5459/1
