On Thu, Mar 26, 2009 at 02:44:37PM +0100, Yegor Yefremov wrote:
> I've looked at SJA1000 and mscan drivers and saw that sja1000.c is using  
> netif_rx(skb) but mscan.c netif_receive_skb(skb) to submit CAN frame.  
> Which should be used?

Depends on what you want. netif_rx() is used for conventional drivers,
netif_receive_skb() is used for NAPI drivers.

IMO, all socketcan drivers should be converted to NAPI sooner or later. Due
to the small packet length, irq rates can be very high. That's exactly what
NAPI was designed for.
