Hi,

I've just added a quick hack to the repository to eliminate conditional
compilation for several kernel versions, i.e. to eliminate the 
#if LINUX_VERSION_CODE >= ... parts, we currently have in our code.

Just call "kv 2.6.16 af_can.c" for example to produce the version of
af_can.c for kernel 2.6.16.  Output is on stdout.
