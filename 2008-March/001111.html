sorry. Indeed i did not want to push you beyond your programming skills ;-)

I just recognized that you only had problems with the SECOND channel and 
that the first channel seems to run properly.

So in that case, i would first try to change the (hardware) connections, 
e.g. attach the busses in a different way to your CAN-card.

And if that's not working, i would try to play with the BIOS settings.
Maybe your parallelport makes more problems when it is disabled.
So enabling and disabling of IRQ reservations and probably iomem 
reservations could also bring some effects.

Sorry this just a bit try-and-error but as the first channel is running 
i would assume the problem to be somewhere in BIOS, IRQ and iomem-range 
settings ...
