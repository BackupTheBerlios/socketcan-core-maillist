Hi,

I think that the wild proliferation of platform .h files is quite a nuisance.

Another advantage about a more rational approach to platform files is
that people will know where to look for basic documentation. When you
are integrating a driver in the kernel for you machine you have at
least to instantiate and fill the platform data. So I would put there
a quick documentation (in kerneldoc style) about about how to do it.
