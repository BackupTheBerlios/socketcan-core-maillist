Hi Wolfgang,

back on duty :-)

I just asked Jens about the clock accuracy and he just pointed to 
standard crystals like:

http://www.jauch.de/ablage/med_00000166_1182240980_prd_00000013_en_SS2.pdf

There you can find a so called "frequency tolerance" measured in ppm (in 
integer values :).

So to be correct we should be able to define a "clock" and a 
"clock_tolerance" for our boards. When a clock_tolerance is given  
(which means !=  0) the bittime algorithm may be able to deal with that. 
When the clock_tolerance is 'unknown' or 'zero' it is fine anyway.

I do not need this tolerance handling in the bittime algorithm right 
now, but we should support it for future use.
