Hi,

Here are two test applications allowing me to generate mscan
order-of-frame bug.
I was using one of the Pengutronix canutils as template.

The canecho_dut is started on the DUT (mscan target) and listens for the
messages to response on them. The ID and all databytes in the response
message are incremented.

The canecho_gen generates the test messages and waits for the responses
from the canecho_dut.

I have not managed to reproduce the problem starting canecho_dut on
mscan0 and canecho_gen on mscan1 (MPC5200B Phytec evaluation board,
customer's 2.6.29.4 kernel built with ELDK 4.1).

The trace is made when canecho_dut runs on mscan0, and canecho_gen on
the separate PC (Ubuntu 9.04) with the SJA1000 card from IXXAT. The
messages 14 and 22 are out of order. Of course, the canecho_gen signals
that problem also.
