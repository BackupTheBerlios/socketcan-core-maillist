Hi, ack for me. Adding error counters to error frames gives much sense
to reporting of bus-errors even on CAN controllers (such the mcp251x)
that don't specify the kind of error (neither if it's a TX or RX one).
An user application inspecting REC and TEC counters can monitor the
"quality" of the bus even on these chips.
