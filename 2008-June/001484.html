> if you know version numbers, it's a piece of cake to add the

I just checked at http://lxr.linux.no. <linux/io.h> seems to exist since
2.6.16. Therefore we need:

#if LINUX_VERSION_CODE >= KERNEL_VERSION(2,6,16)
#include <linux/io.h>
#else
#include <asm/io.h>
#endif
