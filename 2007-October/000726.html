Keep in mind: On a Rev. A chip the MSCAN_CLKSRC_BUS is always used. On a 
Rev. B chip we have the choice and  MSCAN_CLKSRC_XTAL is the preferred 
clock according to the manual. Unfortunately, the mscan driver always 
sets MSCAN_CLKSRC_BUS (to avoid trouble with the old chips).
