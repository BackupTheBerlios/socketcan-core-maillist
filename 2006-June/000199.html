Wolfgang Grandegger wrote:

> 
> I think it does not make sense to store the mode at all as the change 
> might change. What could SIOCGCANMODE be useful for?
> 


The SIOC[SG]CANMODE holds the three basic states a user may want the CAN-Bus

to be:

START
STOP
SLEEP

On the 'reading' side you may also look into SIOCGCANSTATE to get the 
current state of the CAN-bus. But this state may tell you something about 
BUS_WARNING while you are only interested in the information 
START/STOP/SLEEP. So for this reason the SIOCGCANMODE is reasonable.
