Dear SocketCAN users, friends & contributors,

the PF_CAN protocol family is scheduled for the next merge window for 
new Kernel features in Kernel 2.6.25. It has been included in the 
net-2.6.25 developer tree of David Miller (the Linux network maintainer) 
last night.

Thanks to all of you for the constructive feedback & contributions that 
made it possible to bring the SocketCAN concept into the Kernel. It took 
six month to bring the code to a quality that finally had been accepted 
by the networking guys - and it really has been improved in readability 
& stability in this time.

Cheers,
Oliver
